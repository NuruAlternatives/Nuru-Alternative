Lighting the Way to Empowerment

At Nuru Alternatives Limited, we are committed to providing innovative and adaptable solutions to empower rural communities, particularly women. We focus on sustainable energy, water access, household commodities, and farm supplies. Our vision is economically empowered communities leading better lives, and our mission is to enhance affordability and adaptability through innovative solutions.

About Nuru Alternatives Limited

Nuru Alternatives Limited was founded with the mission to transform rural communities by simplifying household tasks, particularly for women who are the cornerstones of these households. Our approach empowers them to engage in other economic and social activities, enhancing the overall well-being of their families.

Our Vision

Our Values

What We Do

We offer a range of products and services designed to address the unique challenges faced by rural communities. Our holistic approach ensures that our solutions cater to the specific needs of each community.

  • Renewable Energy Solutions: We provide solar home systems and solar-powered lanterns to households, helping bridge the gap in rural electrification and reduce reliance on harmful fuels like kerosene.
  • Water Access Projects: We implement rainwater harvesting systems to ensure communities have access to a sustainable water supply, relieving the burden on women and girls who spend hours collecting water.
  • Household Commodities: We offer energy-efficient cooking appliances, mobile phones, radios, and other essential items that improve the quality of life for households.
  • Farm Equipment and Supplies: We collaborate with local artisans to fabricate farm machinery that increases productivity, helping farmers manage their time and resources more effectively.
